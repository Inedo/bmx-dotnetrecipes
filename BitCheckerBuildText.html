To Testing and Beyond

After a build is created and deployed to its first environment (in this case, Integration), there's still a ways to go before production. This is represented in BuildMaster through promotions. Take a look at the "Promotion Status" section below; it shows that this build starts in Integration and then goes to Testing before finally going to Production.

This is a Promotion Workflow, and can be customized in each application. You can also set-up approvals and other promotion requirements as "gateways" to ensure that process is followed between environments. As an example, you'll notice that there is an approval required before the build can be promoted to the Testing environment. If you are logged in as the Admin user, you can go ahead and click the Approve to Testing button. Once you've done that, you are free to hit the Promote to Testing button at any time. Go ahead - give it a shot!
